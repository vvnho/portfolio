CISCO WEBEX MEETINGS FOR ANDROID WEAR

NOTE: The following images are CONCEPT ONLY

As part of an effort to include wearable functionality for Cisco WebEx Meetings for Android in version 8.6, I spent a week researching Android Wear patterns and conventions, as well as completing a first draft of wireframes for basic, key functions on the watch.

NOTIFICATION DRIVEN

The practicality of Android Wear lies primarily in responding to notifications on your wrist. When a meeting is about to begin, a notification will allow you to join the meeting immediately. Once you're in the meeting, you can connect your audio, mute or unmute your microphone, view the participant list, or exit the meeting from the watch.

USER DEMAND DRIVEN

If there are no notifications present, you can still access primary functions, such as viewing a list of your upcoming meetings or entering your personal meeting room, in one of two ways: through voice commands or launching the app from the drawer.
