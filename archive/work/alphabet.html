ALPHABET BOOK

A series of nine letterforms created out of Wrigley's 5® gum wrappers. Letterforms were created through slicing, folding, arranging, and sculpting of the gum wrappers. These nine letterforms where arranged in a 3x3 grid composition with visual flow in mind.

Consequently, I designed a book in a five-part series with other designers who pursued exploring typographic form from everyday objects.
