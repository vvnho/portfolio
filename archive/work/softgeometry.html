SOFT GEOMETRY

The first in the series is a replica of Mathieu Lehanneur's Soft Geometry Pendant Lamp. The rest of the lighting fixtures in the series are thus inspired by Lehanneur's in their use of both geometric and organic forms and how they interact with each other.

Drawn and Rendered in VectorWorks
Retouched in Photoshop

INFORMATIONAL BROCHURE/POSTER

An introduction to each of the lighting fixtures in the series in a poster/booklet hybrid. Each light is detailed in elevation, complete with dimensions and a short description. The booklet then can be unfolded into a poster with the lights rendered in a 3-dimensional space.

Project Specifications:

  • Folded Dimensions: 4.25" x 5.5"
  • Open Dimensions: 11" x 17"
