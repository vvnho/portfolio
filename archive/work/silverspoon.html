SILVER SPOON

A college friend of mine is starting a small business in food delivery and asked me to join the project as the team's first official designer.

LOGO

I played around with a lot of different shapes and typefaces before reaching this combination. The script type gives the logo a luxe feel that isn't too dated while the varied scalloped shape is both elegant and playful.

Logo process PDF here.

WEBSITE

Although the website is pretty simple in nature, I thought there to be three main points that will directly affect the success of this small business:

1. The website must "wow" the first time buyer
2. It should be dead simple to order items
3. The food photography must be gorgeous (related to #1)

With these points in mind, I designed a simple, straightforward website with just enough animation to provide delight to the user.
