CISCO WEBEX MEETINGS FOR ANDROID

From April-July 2016, I rehauled the entire visual design for Cisco Webex Meetings on the Android platform. The updated version (9.5 release) of the mobile app was live in the Google Play Store in Sept 2016. I covered all of the Android platform (both phone and tablet) and helped my partner with a small portion of the iOS platform.

On this project, I collaborated with:

  • UX designers to understand the interaction updates
  • Engineering teams to ensure that the builds they were coding were matching the UI that I had created
  • PMs to communicate the new UI changes
  • my iOS counterpart and the Desktop designers to maintain consistency throughout our application
