    Bio Brew opened in Fall 2005 and is run by Sodexho. Their drink selection includes, coffee, espresso drinks, Tazo teas, Island Oasis smoothies, and bottled drinks. They also serve food items such as pastries, sandwiches, cookies, salads, chips, and more. This location is pretty popular amongst students who have class in the building despite most of the classrooms not allowing food inside.

    Cargo Coffee is a coffee stand business that is privately owned by a local couple. Their three stands offer the usual: a variety of drip coffees, espresso drinks, and fruit juices. They also serve a decent variety of breakfast pastries, bagels, and quiches (supplied from the Bella Bru Cafe in Sacramento). In the winter months, they also serve soup.

    On April 30, 2005, the stand at King Hall burned down at about 6 o'clock in the morning. It was a suspicious event and the fire investigators spent the day inspecting the incident. It happened to be the first day of finals for the law students so rumor has it that the law students set the stand on fire in some kind of protest.

    In February 2011, the King Hall stand was replaced with a new trailer and has continued to serve coffee since.

    The South Cafe at the Student Community Center is a small extension of the CoHo at the Memorial Union. They offer mostly the same things as the coffee side of Swirlz Bakery. In addition, the South Cafe has a signature item, the Chili Stack. The Chili Stack is chili layered with slices of cornbread, a family favorite of the main food service manager at the CoHo.
