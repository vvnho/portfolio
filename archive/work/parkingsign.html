PARKING SIGN REDESIGN

Given a confusing and cluttered parking sign that exists along Mariposa Street in San Francisco, the goal was to redesign and improve the sign. This sign indicates when drivers could and could not park during the baseball season. The sign is difficult to read at a distance and is too complicated to understand quickly.

I explored other ways to show days of the month by researching different calendar designs. In addition, I created several iterations of drafts and also conducted user testing.
