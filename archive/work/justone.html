JUST ONE

Just One is a fictitious company that sells instant cake mix. Just One is specifically a single serving of instant cake, which sets it apart from other similar products on the market. The customer would simply empty the contents of the packet into a mug, add water, and microwave for a couple of minutes before a single serving of cake is ready to be enjoyed.

I designed the logo and branding for this company. In addition, I designed a complete business system and graphic identity manual.

I created a sustainable packaging prototype as a gift set or starter kit of the Just One product. A porcelain mug is included with a mixing spoon and 5 individual packets of cake mix. The box is intended to be constructed from flower seed paper in an effort to be more sustainable. The box is an oblong hexagon in reference to the shape of sugar molecules. I also designed a pattern based on the frosting section of the logo and printed a scarf. Such a scarf could be used as a promotional piece or general merchandise.
