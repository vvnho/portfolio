DECONSTRUCT THE GLASS CEILING

BACKGROUND

This ficitional social campaign hopes to shed light on gender bias within the modern workplace, exposing its roots to deconstruct the glass ceiling that currently separates women from men. In this way, the campaign can positively promote gender equality, coming together as a community to raise awareness of everyone's unconscious biases.

A variety of different promotional collateral and advertisments were created for this campaign in order to spread awareness to the public. I designed a simple web portal for people to learn more about the campaign and also a short motion graphic informational video for distribution throughout social media to increase our web presence.

In collaboration with:
Trisha Kietikul & Helen H.
