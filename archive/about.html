Hello! I'm Vivian, a designer from the golden state of California. I just recently graduated from the Univeristy of California, Davis, majoring in Design (with an emphasis in Visual Communication) and minoring in Computer Science.

I believe that with correct and proper storytelling, all design problems can be solved. The problem can be framed and projected in its entirety only after an extensive amount of background research on the project has been completed. From there, the full potential and range of possibilities are open for exploration and design iterations.
